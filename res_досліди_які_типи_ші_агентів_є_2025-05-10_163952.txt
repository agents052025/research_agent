Comprehensive Research Report on AI Agents

Summary of Findings:
AI agents are categorized based on capabilities such as reflex actions, goal orientation, utility consideration, and learning abilities. They serve various functional roles across industries, enhancing automation and decision-making processes.

Key Insights:
- Simple reflex agents operate on immediate perception without internal state.
- Model-based reflex agents maintain internal state to handle different scenarios.
- Goal-based agents act to achieve defined goals, improving decision-making.
- Utility-based agents optimize their performance based on a utility function.
- Learning agents improve their actions based on historical data and experiences.
